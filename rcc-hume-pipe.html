RCC Hume Pipe

Reinforced Cement Concrete (RCC) Hume Pipes are essential in modern infrastructure for drainage, sewage, and irrigation systems. They are known for their strength, durability, and resistance to environmental conditions, making them ideal for various applications. Below, we explore the different types, thicknesses, and uses of RCC Hume Pipes.

Thickness :

25 mm, 75 mm, 150 mm

Applications :

Drainage and Large, Small Sewers

Explore Different Types

01

Non-Pressure (NP) Hume Pipes

Designed for non-pressure applications such as drainage and irrigation.

02

Light Duty (P1) Hume Pipes

Panels with hollow cores to reduce weight and improve insulation. Residential buildings, office partitions, and temporary walls.

03

Pipes with Spigot and Socket Joints

Feature spigot and socket ends for easy jointing and sealing.
