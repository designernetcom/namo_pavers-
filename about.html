Innovative Paving Solutions for Every Space

Namo Pavers & Designer Tiles is a leading manufacturer of high-quality pavers and tiles, serving the construction and landscaping industries for over two decades. Established in 2002 by a team of experienced professionals, Namo Pavers began with a small manufacturing facility and a grand vision. Today, we operate with a manufacturing capacity of 225,000 square feet per month, committed to innovation, sustainability, and customer satisfaction. Our diverse product range includes concrete pavers, clay pavers, permeable pavers for driveways, walkways, patios, and pool decks, along with decorative accents, edging stones, and retaining wall blocks. All our products undergo rigorous testing and meet ISI criteria to ensure the highest quality for large- scale projects.

Ritesh's Career Journey

Ritesh began his career in the tile industry in 1995. Over the next thirty years, he gained invaluable experience in sales, marketing, and product development. His passion and dedication have been pivotal in building Namo Pavers into a reputable manufacturer. The Birth of Namo Pavers & Designer Tiles

With a vision to revolutionize the tile industry through quality, sustainability, and innovation, Ritesh established Namo Pavers with a dedicated team. Today, Namo is recognized for excellence in manufacturing high-quality pavers and tiles.
